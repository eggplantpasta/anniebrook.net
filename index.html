Landscapes

Designers and Builders of Beautiful Gardens.

Since 1993, Anniebrook Landscapes has been dedicated to designing and building beautiful gardens. Working for companies such as Waldecks Nurseries, the Vines Golf Course Estate, Curtin University, and Radisson Hotels and Resorts, we have gained the knowledge and experience in designing, building and maintaining some of Australia?s most exceptional gardens.

Anniebrook Landscapes has designed and built luxury residential gardens in - Hillarys, South Perth, Como, Bunbury, Dalyellup, Port Geographe, Busselton, Dunsborough, Eagle Bay, Yallingup, Margaret River and Augusta

We have also completed commercial projects for - West One Apartments, West Perth; Geographe Bayview Resort, Busselton; Cape View Apartments, Busseltonin Dunsborough, Yallingup, Eagle Bay, Augusta and Perth.

Design

We pride ourselves on artistic and unique designs for each and every garden. With over 20 years of landscape design behind us, Anniebrook Landscapes has experience with projects ranging in size from large commercial through to luxury residential.

Construction

Anniebrook Landscapes is a fully equipped and insured landscape construction business. Working closely with a team of skilled tradesmen, we can provide a full range of landscape services.

Maintenance

To help your garden become all it can be, Anniebrook Landscapes has a team of skilled horticulturists who can design a maintenance programme specifically for your garden.
